About Us

History

The establishment of Chuka University was foretold war back in 1951 by a famous philanthropist and seer by the name Jerushaa Kanyua. she prophesied that an academic mountain shall sprout at Ndugani

Mission

To provide access genaerate, preserve and share knowledge for quality, effective and ethical leadership in higher education, training, research and outreach through nurturing an intellectual culture that integrates theory with practice, innovation and enterpreneurship.

Vision

A premier univesity for the provision of quality education, trainig and research for sustainable national and global development.

Philosophy

Quality education, training, research, extension, enivornimental sustainablity, and enterpreneurship lead to social cohesion, human integrity, and economic development.
